COMMITTEES

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

Agenda: The Issue of use of Chemical Weapons in Syria

UNSC is charged with the maintenance of international peace and security and approving any changes in the United Nations Charter. The UNSC is an implementing body and has plethora of other functions. The situation in Syria is very severe and quick action needs be taken to improve it.


United Nations General Assembly
Disarmament and International Security Committee
(UNGA DISEC)

Agenda: Preventing Weaponization of Space.

The first committee of the UNGA, UNGA DISEC deals primarily with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affects the international community and seeks solutions to challenges in the international security regime. Today the arms race is developing in the space too and is a threat to the human race. Proper measures should be taken to minimize the risk.


World Health Organization (WHO)

Agenda: Role of Civil Society, NGOs and Private Sector in Tackling Infectious Diseases.

WHO is a specialized agency of the UN that is concerned with public health. Its primary goal is to direct international health within the UN’s system. With the outbreak of diseases all over the world NGOs and Private Sectors play an important role in curbing such crises.


Human Rights Council (HRC)

Agenda: Discussing Human Rights Violation in Balochistan.

The Human Rights Council is an inter-governmental body within the United Nations, strengthening promotion and protection of human rights and addressing situations of human rights violation, around the globe. The agenda on the floor would be to discuss the human rights violations in Balochistan due to its current situation and to find optimum solutions.


Loksabha

Agenda: To Build Consensus Between Political Parties for Construction of Ram Temple in Ayodhya(Ram Janmabhoomi).

The lower house of the Parliament of India, Loksabha consists of representatives elected directly by the people of India. TheAyodhya dispute is a political, historical and socio-religious debate in India, centered on a plot of land in the city ofAyodhya, located inFaizabad district,Uttar Pradesh. The main issues revolve around access to a site traditionally regarded amongHindus to be the birthplace of the Hindu deityRama, the history and location of theBabri Mosque at the site, and whether a previous Hindu temple was demolished or modified to create the mosque.


Historic Crisis Committee

Agenda: Discussion on The Constitution (42nd Amendment) Act (National Emergency) Declared by Indira Gandhi on Controversial Grounds in 1975.

The Historic Crisis Committee is a special and unique committee of LITMUN’18 which stimulates the situation of a historic crisis and allows the delegates to discuss and come out with new solutions on the past events. The agenda of this committee revolves around the National Emergency imposed in 1975 by Indira Gandhi.
